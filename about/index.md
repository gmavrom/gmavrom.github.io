## A few words about me

<img align="left" src="http://mavromatidis.me/downloads/Mavromatidis_circle.png"  width="170" style="margin: 15px 20px 20px 0px">

Hi, my name is Georgios and I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Chair of Building Physics, ETH Zurich.

I hold a PhD degree from ETH Zurich in the field of building and urban energy systems. My thesis focused on the optimal design of distributed urban energy systems under uncertainty. Before my PhD studies, I obtained an MSc in Sustainable Energy Futures from Imperial College London and a Diploma in Mechanical Engineering from Aristotle University of Thessaloniki.
