Hi, my name is Georgios and I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Chair of Building Physics, ETH Zurich.

I hold a PhD degree from ETH Zurich in the field of building and urban energy systems. My thesis focused on the optimal design of distributed urban energy systems under uncertainty. Before my PhD, I graduated from [Aristotle University of Thessaloniki][] with a MEng in [Mechanical Engineering][]. I continued my path at [Imperial College London][] from where I graduated with an [MSc in Sustainable Energy Futures][].
